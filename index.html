Ilja Kuzborskij


I am currently a postdoctoral researcher working with Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi.

Previously, I finished PhD at EPFL and Idiap Research Institute, advised by Barbara Caputo and Francesco Orabona.
During my PhD studies I also did a number of internships and scientific visits: Google Brain Zürich (2017 Oct - 2018 Feb) with Olivier Bousquet, IST Austria (2016 Oct - 2017 Apr) with Christoph Lampert, Toyota Technological Insititute at Chicago (Summer 2013) with Francesco Orabona.

My current research interest is in data-dependent analysis of learning algorithms. In particular I am interested in analysis of deep learning (specifically, implicit regularization in the optimization algorithms), stochastic optimization, online learning, nonparametric prediction, and efficient forms of transfer learning.
