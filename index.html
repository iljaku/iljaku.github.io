Ilja Kuzborskij


I am a research scientist at DeepMind, London.

I am interested in a problem-dependent (distribution/sample/instance-dependent) analysis and design of learning algorithms in statistical, non-stochastic online, partial feedback, and nonparametric prediction models.

Previously, I spent one great year as a postdoc with Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi. Before that I finished PhD at EPFL and Idiap Research Institute, advised by Barbara Caputo and Francesco Orabona. During my PhD studies I also did a number of internships and scientific visits: Google Brain Zürich (2017 Oct - 2018 Feb) with Olivier Bousquet, IST Austria (2016 Oct - 2017 Apr) with Christoph Lampert, Toyota Technological Insititute at Chicago (Summer 2013) with Francesco Orabona.

You can contact me at firstname.lastname@gmail.com
